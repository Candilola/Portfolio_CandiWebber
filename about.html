Hi, I'm Candi

A UX/UI designer with extensive experience in graphic design. Working primarily in media, I am highly organised and detail oriented, with a passion and drive to deliver user focused design. I strive to understand the user through proactive research and usability testing. Specialising in information architecture, wireframing and prototyping, I create interfaces that increase brand loyalty and user engagement. A collaborative professional, I am a and player with experience working in large organisations and small scale environments, as well as remote working.

I love to create pleasing, simple designs, I have a talent for organising content and whipping it into beautiful, elegant, user-focused interfaces, understanding the user is at the heart of everything I do.
